Welcome to the **SoloSuccess AI** engineering blog.

I'm building this entire platform as a solo founder, but I'm not doing it alone. I've assembled a team of **internal AI agents** to help me scale my development velocity.

## My AI Team

- **Social Media Manager:** Automatically drafts and posts updates to X based on my git commits.
- **Code Generator:** Scaffolds new features and components using my design system.
- **Code Reviewer:** Acts as my second pair of eyes, catching bugs before they hit production.
- **Repo Committer:** Keeps my git history clean with semantic commit messages.

## What's Next?

I'm currently working on a **Blog Writer Agent** that will help me document this journey by drafting these very posts. The goal is to share the technical challenges, the wins, and the lessons learned while building a SaaS product from scratch.

Stay tuned for more updates!
